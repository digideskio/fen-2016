Front-end North 2016

A full-day conference for front-end developers, designers and people who make things for the web on September 30th, 2016.

Returning from a year's hiatus after our successful debut in 2014 we can't wait to bring the Northern front-end development community together for another great day of learning, insight and inspiration!
